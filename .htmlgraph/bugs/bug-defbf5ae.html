Commit trailer parser in reindex_trailers.go only recognizes feat-, bug-, spk-, trk- prefixes. Refs: pln-abc and Refs: spc-abc trailers are silently discarded during reindex. Plans and specs are first-class work item types. Fix: add pln- and spc- to isWorkItemID.
